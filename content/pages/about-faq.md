# About page FAQ

1. **Is this the same as reading my horoscope?**
   No. A horoscope guesses what might happen today for an entire sign. A reading works from your exact chart to explain the patterns already running in your life — why, not what's next.

2. **Will you tell me what's going to happen?**
   No — and be skeptical of any reading that claims to. I can show you the pattern behind the question you're carrying. What you do with it is yours to decide.

3. **What if I don't believe in astrology?**
   You don't need to. Think of the chart as a structured way to talk about patterns you already sense in yourself — belief isn't a prerequisite for the conversation to be useful.

4. **How is this different from therapy?**
   It isn't a replacement for it. I'm not a therapist, and this isn't clinical treatment — it's a different lens, one you might bring into therapy, a journal, or your own reflection.

5. **Are the archetypes (like "Guardian" or "Builder") fixed personality types?**
   No — they're patterns you're currently running, not permanent labels. The same chart can show a different archetype rising depending on the season of life you're in.

6. **What do you need from me to book a session?**
   Your exact birth date, time, and place. Time matters more than people expect — it sets your rising sign and house placements, and without it a chart can only say so much.

7. **Do you keep what I share private?**
   Yes. If a session ever informs something I write, it's anonymized and reshaped enough that it isn't recognizable — the pattern matters, not your personal details.
